Let’s look at the "Portrait of Giovanni Arnolfini and his Wife"


Jan van Eyck's 1434 masterpiece, "Portrait of Giovanni Arnolfini and his Wife," is a pivotal Dutch painting showing the wealthy Arnolfini and his wife, possibly during their marriage ceremony, in Bruges. Celebrated for its intricate detail and symbolic elements, the work showcases van Eyck’s revolutionary oil painting skills.
